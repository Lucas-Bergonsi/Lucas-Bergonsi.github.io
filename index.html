Biography

Lucas Bergonzi has a master's degree in Physics and is enthusiastic about Mechanical Engineering. He is currently studying programming languages for applications in physics, engineering, and data science.

He started his studies at Escola Técnica Parobé in the mechanical technical course. Later, he graduated in Physics from the Federal University of Santa Maria, concluding in 2018. The obtaining of this degree was followed by a post-graduation in Physics, at the master's level, from the Federal University of Pelotas, where he specialized in thin films physics and scientific instrumentation.
